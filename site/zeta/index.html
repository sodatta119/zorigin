What it'll do

Low-latency input over your Wi-Fi.

01

Wireless trackpad

Move the cursor, tap to click, two-finger scroll - a laptop trackpad in your hand.

02

Keyboard input

Type on the computer from your phone, including shortcuts and modifier keys.

03

Low-latency LAN

Input travels over your local network, so it feels immediate - not a laggy cloud round-trip.

04

No screen streaming

Zeta sends input, not video - so it stays light, fast and private.

05

Any computer

A small helper on Mac / Windows / Linux; your phone connects with a QR or link.

The honest bit

Zeta moves input, not your screen - that's what keeps it fast and private. It needs a small helper running on the computer and both devices on the same Wi-Fi; pairing is the same QR / link model as the rest of zOrigin.
