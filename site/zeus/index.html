What it'll do

Wake-on-LAN and SSH, over your own network.

01

Saved devices

Name each machine with its MAC and network - ready to wake in one tap.

02

Wake-on-LAN

Sends a magic packet over the LAN. Works on every platform, nothing to install on the PC.

03

Up / down status

A quick ping tells you whether a machine is awake right now.

04

Restart & shutdown

Power commands over SSH, with credentials kept in your device's secure keychain - never in plaintext.

05

Widgets & quick tiles

Wake or sleep a machine from your home screen, without opening the app.

The honest bit

Zeus stays LAN-only - it won't relay power commands over the internet. It speaks the standard Wake-on-LAN and SSH your machines already do, so there's nothing extra to install on the PC. Your SSH keys live in the OS keychain and are only ever used to reach your own machines.
