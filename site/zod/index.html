What it'll do

A fast, private inventory of your LAN.

01

Device list

Every device with IP, hostname, MAC and vendor - plus when you first and last saw it.

02

Ping & latency

Round-trip time to any device on the network.

03

Port scan

A quick TCP scan per device shows which services are open.

04

Link monitoring

Periodic speed, latency and packet-loss samples on a timeline.

05

Details

Reverse DNS, service labels and best-effort OS hints.

The honest bit

Full scanning is Android + desktop; iOS locks down the raw access a scanner needs, so it'll be limited there - and we'll say so. Fing already owns this space; Zod's edge isn't more features, it's speed, a clean UI, and no account or cloud telemetry.
