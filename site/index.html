Philosophy

Built on a few stubborn principles.

01

Local-first

Your files and data stay on your own network. No cloud relay, no servers we operate, nothing to leak.

02

No accounts

Nothing to sign up for. Pair two devices with a QR or a link and you're done.

03

No fragile discovery

We never rely on mDNS, BLE or multicast - the exact things home routers quietly break. That's why it works when others don't.

04

Honest engineering

Speed is your Wi-Fi's real speed, not marketing. We tell you the limits - including where the OS gets in the way.

The engine

One shared core under every tool.

Every zOrigin app is built on the same foundation - explicit pairing, a lean LAN transport, resumable and verified transfers. Reliability isn't a feature we bolt on; it's the whole idea.

pair  →  QR / URL / one-time token  (never mDNS or BLE)
transport  →  plain HTTP on the LAN, streamed, resumable
verify  →  crc32 on arrival  - byte-exact or it retries
privacy  →  nothing leaves the subnet
